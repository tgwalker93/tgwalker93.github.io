About Me


A lion is a very aggressive animal. I like how they care so much about territory and like to establish dominance. They travel in packs, and they are extremely strong. I like watching them take down animals twice their size on the Discovery channel.


Lions have amazing teeth, very sharp and to the point. Their head hair looks intense, definitely gives them the king vibe. Hence their name "King of the jungle"
